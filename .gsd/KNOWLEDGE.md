# Knowledge

Append-only register of project-specific rules, patterns, and lessons learned.

## K001 — Contest submission formats
- The contest accepts: video clip, motion graphic, podcast, slide presentation, digital comic/infographic
- We submit as: web link (via GitHub Pages) + screen-recording video walkthrough
- AI usage must be declared with checkboxes on the registration form

## K002 — Target age group
- Son is grade 8 (~14 years old), fits the 13–17 age group
- Content and puzzles should be age-appropriate, educational, engaging

## K003 — Historical accuracy sources
- Primary: "Kể chuyện Điện Biên Phủ" (NXB Kim Đồng)
- Visual reference: "Ký họa trong chiến hào" (Drawing Under Fire)
- The 6 historical figures to feature: Hồ Chí Minh, Dân Công (collective), Võ Nguyên Giáp, Phan Đình Giót, Tô Vĩnh Diện, Lá Cờ Chiến Thắng (symbolic)
